ISA Events

Holi

Holi is the marquee event for us in the Spring Semester. The festival of colors is celebrated as grand as it is celebrated back in India and is enjoyed by people from every part of the world. You will get to adorn with colors and water while we’ll have a DJ playing some Bollywood and Western music in the background. The festival is enhanced by serving a full Indian meal to make sure they experience and savour rich Indian delicacies. We have been having an attendance of 1200 for this event for the past few years. This the event you cannot afford to miss.

ASU HOLI 2018 got nominated for Pitchfork Awards 2018 under the categories ‘Outstanding recurring program’ and ‘Outstanding Cultural, diversity & Inclusion celebration Program

Bollywood Night

Bollywood Night is an ice-breaker event for the students arrived here for the Spring semester. There would be interesting activities at the beginning of the event to promote the Indian Film Industry and we will have music from a Live DJ playing Bollywood and English songs as the dance floor would be opened to people to groove and show some moves. In this way, students can interact with new people and get to know more about their cultures which ASU focuses on. Food would be served as well to make sure that the attendees go home with a full stomach. This is because the end time of the event is almost dinner time and after losing a lot of calories due to dancing, the attendees would surely look to hog on some delicious pizzas. It will truly be a fun filled event for every student involved as they dance, sing and click photos with their friends as well as make new friends all through the night.

Republic Day

The event is the celebration of the Republic Day of India. We at ISA, plan to involve all the students to witness the significance of the day as well the celebrations that are held on the day. The day would involve unfolding of the Indian tricolor followed by a musical performance by some of the students. Also, we would have a screening of the parade held at New Delhi, India which portrays the different regions of India as well as the Indian Army, Navy and Air Force. In this way, we let everyone know more about India, the culture and the people.

Jhankaar

Jhankaar has always been the Indian Students Association’s most anticipated annual cultural event which has been growing every year. Starting in 2007, Jhankaar has been the extravaganza that captivates and binds the diverse student culture and showcases the best talents of ASU. The event will be filled with dance performances of different styles, music of various genres, mad ads, stand up comedy and many more. To end the event at a higher note, there are a variety of fun games and Indian food for everyone to enjoy.

Jhankaar won the Pitchfork award 2017 for Best Entertainment Program over 500 attendees. It was nominated for Pitchfork Award 2018 for Outstanding Entertainment Program over 500 attendees and Outstanding Cultural, Diversity, and Inclusion Celebration Program.
